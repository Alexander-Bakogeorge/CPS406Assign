About us


Welcome to Cypress by EzSoftware. Our mission is to provide a way for citizens of the beautiful city of Toronto to virtually report a problem within the city by pinpointing their location on a user-friendly map. Cypress is by us, and for the community. We hope to establish a better environment in which the friendly community can come together and make the city a better place by reporting their problems and concerns - allowing the council of the city of Toronto to view these problems and eventually deploy an efficient fix. Cypress is the future. It eliminates the need for endless phone calls to the city, and broken communication when trying to solve a public concern.
